About

Button Blazer is a challenging game developed by Perfect Corner. It provides a great way to challenge your friends and become the best!

Developed by two 15 year olds from Plano, Texas, this app will surely pack a challenge and keep you wanting more!

Originally starting out as a quick winter break project to learn about the basics of coding, the app eventually turned in to a full fledged app, and, on Feb 6th, it was first released for android. A few weeks later the app was subsequently released on iOS, seeing international growth.
